Who Is Jackie Bishop?

My name is Jackie and I am a front end web developer and designer. I live about twenty minutes outside of Philadelphia in the quaint little town of Beverly NJ.

After graduating art school with a major in photography and a minor in graphic design, I decided to push myself further and bring my design knowledge to the web development table. Over the years I have aquired a very special set of skills including:

  • 15 years of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator experience
  • A fantastic sense of design and composition
  • An in-depth knowledge of workflow and time management
  • The ability to work well with others towards a common goal
  • The art of listening to what people really want and need


About Me

When I graduated college, I got a job working for an E-Commerce business as their main photographer and graphic designer. To lighten the workload when my coworkers called out, my boss taught me a bit about various computer languages so I could fill in where needed.

I grew to love the work and decided to further both my knowledge and my career. Web development became a passion for me and I enjoy using all the knowledge I have gained to develop websites that are not only functional, but also elegant and beautiful.

When I am not coding or designing, you'll catch me gardening with the kids, gaming with my hubby, or fixing up our humble little home.
